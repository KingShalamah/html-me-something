The Reason I Became Interested In Computers

For a while, I knew absolutely nothing about computers. As a matter of fact, I did not even have an email address until the
autumn season of 2005, and I rarely, if ever used it. However, one day while walking through Best Buy, in the musical
instrument section, I saw some computer software that claimed to be able to help musicians create music.

Ever since that day, my interest in computers has grown continuously, to include:

  • Buying and using music software
  • Composing and recording songs from scratch, not sampling beats
  • Learning web programming such as PHP / MySQL (self taught) - I joined Launch Code for formal help, so I can keep what hair I have left :-)
  • Other topics which dive further into the internet

Friends Once Laughed, Now They Can't Keep Up

Some of my friends used to laugh or sound surprised because I did not even have an email address until the autumn or winter season of 2005.
Plus, I did not even know how to unzip a file on Windows. That was back in the days when you had to buy a special piece of software just
to unzip compressed zip files.

However, once I built a simple, but working miniature social network in PHP and MySQL, without any formal training, they were no longer
laughing, though some of them had degrees in the IT field.

That's why I believe anyone who truly desires to learn a new skill should go for it.
That's why I love LaunchCode, because they are helping me and several others to do just that!
